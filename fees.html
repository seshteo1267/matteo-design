What We Offer

Services

Service One

Stylist Fee for
Private Clients

Transform your living space into a photo-worthy setting — whether by rearranging your current furniture, layering in antiques, or enhancing with carefully sourced decorative objects.

Fees are determined by the size of the space, the quantity of rooms, and the depth of sourcing required. Every project begins with a complimentary consultation.

Request a Consultation

What's Included

Complimentary 30-min Consultation

Virtual walkthrough via FaceTime to discuss your space, goals, and vision before any commitment.

Photo Submission & Scope

You share photos of the space; we document the full scope of work together before beginning.

On-Site Assessment

Full inventory of existing furnishings with an eye for what stays, what moves, and what's needed.

Sourcing & Purchasing

Hunting for the right objects, art, or accents — including antiques and one-of-a-kind finds.

Returns & Final Alignment

Processing of any unused items and final styling to ensure the space reflects your vision entirely.

Service Two

Photo Shoot Styling
for Professionals

For architects and interior designers preparing projects for print publications, websites, and social media. Meticulous preparation meets collaborative on-set execution.

Fees are structured by half-day or full-day scheduling, with pricing tailored to the scope of the shoot. Contact us to discuss your project.

Start a Conversation

What's Included

Pre-Shoot Consultation

Collaborative planning session with the designer to establish the creative vision, mood, and shot list.

Prop & Décor Selection

Assistance selecting and acquiring the props, accessories, and décor that bring the shoot to life.

On-Set Photographer Coordination

Close collaboration with the photographer on the day to ensure angles, compositions, and styling all align.

Finishing Touches

The candlesticks, the artwork, the final details — all the elements that complete the designer's vision.
