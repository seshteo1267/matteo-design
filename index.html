About the Studio

The art of making a home feel complete.

Get in Touch

Matteo & Co. brings a refined eye and a passion for the details that transform a room from simply furnished to truly finished. Whether collaborating with architects and designers on photo shoot day, or helping a homeowner see their space with fresh perspective — every project is approached with the same care and intention.

Founded by Tamera Matteo, the studio specializes in the quiet work that makes great spaces unforgettable: the perfect arrangement, the unexpected accent, the finishing touch that makes everything click.
